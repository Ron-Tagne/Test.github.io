About Me

I am a Master of Public Health student also completing a Certificate in Data Science and hold a Bachelor of Health Information Management. I have passion for using data to improve the health and well-being of communities and individuals around the world.


I have a strong background in statistics, epidemiology and health informatics, as well as experience working with large, complex datasets. I am well-versed in using data analysis and data visualisation tools such as R and RStudio, Python and Jupyter Notebook/ Google Collaborate, Microsoft Excel, SQL and Tableau. My current focus is on using data to understand and address public health issues such as infectious disease outbreaks, chronic disease, and health disparities. I have experience in utilizing electronic health records and public datasets and employing machine learning techniques to uncover patterns and predict outcomes.


I am a strong collaborator and enjoy working with other professionals from diverse backgrounds including epidemiologists, clinicians, and policymakers. I am always open to discussing new opportunities to work together to improve public health outcomes.


Thank you for visiting my website. Please feel free to reach out if you have any questions or if you would like to discuss a potential collaboration.
